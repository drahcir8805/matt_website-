Cursor Hackathon

- Create a portfolio of Matts works
- Then a big book now button
  - User clicks book now and sees application form asking for:
    - Full name
    - Email
    - Date of event
- Once that is all done user is taken to a chatbot page where the chatbot knows all about Matt’s pricing standards and prompts the user to say what he wants
  - Example 2 hours of photography coverage, 2 hours video coverage and then 30 photos edited after plus all raw photos
- And then depending on what the user needs matts chatbot will give a quote of the pricing
  - We can also have matt verify just the price and the needs first before the chatbot sends it off but only for scenarios where the AI doesn’t know what price to give for the request for example “I want you to use our own film and digital camera and post process all the photos as well” or “I want you to come before the wedding to shoot BTS”
- After the quote is confirmed the chatbot will create an invoice and send it to the user
- The user will then pay through stripe
